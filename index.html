    Games

    MIUSOFT is a startup indie game studio that creates enjoyable games for both PC and mobile devices. Our goal is to provide you with games that you'll love and that will keep you entertained for hours on end. Currently, we specialize in 2D games, but we are already working on expanding our skills and moving into 3D game development.

    We would be thrilled if you checked out our games and gave us your feedback! Your thoughts and opinions are incredibly valuable to us, as they help us shape the future of our games. Join us on our journey of creating amazing games that you'll absolutely love to play!

    Design

    Before embarking on my journey as a programmer, I worked as a graphic designer. Over the years, I have completed numerous design projects for clients both locally and internationally. While I'm not currently working on design projects professionally, I still create vector templates for stock websites as a hobby.

    As a game developer, I take pride in designing all aspects of my games, from icons to promotional posts. I enjoy having creative control over my projects, and my background in graphic design has been a valuable asset in this regard.

    If you're interested in seeing some of my designs or hearing what my customers have to say about me, please check out my freelancer profile. You'll find feedback from satisfied clients, and you'll get a sense of my design style and capabilities. I hope to continue to create visually stunning designs for both my games and any future design projects!
